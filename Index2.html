What Am I?

Squirrels are members of the family Sciuridae, consisting of small or medium-size rodents. The family includes tree squirrels, ground squirrels, chipmunks, marmots, flying squirrels, and prairie dogs. The living squirrels are divided into five subfamilies, with about 58 genera and some 285 species.
